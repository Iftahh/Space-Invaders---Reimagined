Welcome to my JS13K game

It is a Javascript game under 13kb (when zipped), built in one month (Aug. 13th to Sep. 13th), for a fun contest.
The theme this year is "The Elements: Earth, Water, Air and Fire".

My plan is to build a turn-based, two-player, capture-the-flag, with jet-packs and destructable ground (ie. like Worms or Scortched Earth)

Use space to fire the Jetpack, left and right arrows to move.

Click the screenshot or here to play.
